Who made Titan Tv man

The Titan TV Man is a Titan created by the TV Men to aid The Alliance in the war against the Skibidi Toilets. The Titan TV Man is around 100-300 feet in height, wears a giant black trench, black jeans with black gloves, it has spikes on its back that can stab Skibidi Toilets or infected Alliance members, and has a purple core. There seems to be black smoke around Titan TV Man when it makes a first appearance and makes a strange sound before starting up its red-light attack. This Titan was presumed to be the strongest out of all, as it is seen in its first appearance in Episode 41 absolutely annihilating a whole division of Skibidi Toilets by using its special hypnotic red light to completely disrupt them and making them flush themselves.

Attack

When activated, a red light will shine tremendously while playing the THX sound effect, causing any toilets nearby to "flush" themselves because of the annoying sound.

Damage

High (It's red light attack depends on the target, and how many targets there are. It can cause Skibidi Toilets to commit suicide by flushing themselves with it's red light. It can stab the target with it's blades, doing beefy damage or it can kick the target with it's foot, also doing beefy damage.)

Durability

Extremely High (This character has demonstrated immense durability in Episode 44, when he is left undamaged after being hit with 2 rockets.)

Speed

Average (This character has demonstrated decent speed in Episode 44, where he punched a toilet's glasses off swiftly.)

Height

100-300 feet tall
